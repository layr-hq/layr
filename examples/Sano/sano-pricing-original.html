Frequently asked questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Talk to sales.

What counts as an event?

Any user action you track in Sano — page views, button clicks, sign-ups, custom events. Identify calls and group updates do not count toward your monthly limit.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Upgrades are prorated; downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my event limit?

We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limit. We never drop events on Pro and Enterprise — overages are billed at $0.50 per 1,000 events. Free and Starter plans pause ingestion until the next month or upgrade.

Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits?

Yes. Eligible early-stage startups get 50% off Pro for 12 months, and registered non-profits get the Pro plan for free. Apply via our partnerships page.

How does annual billing work?

Pay once per year and save 20% versus monthly. You can switch back to monthly billing at the end of your annual term.

Is my data secure?

Sano is SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise customers can choose US or EU data residency, and HIPAA-compliant deployments are available.
