# Default Bias

## What it is
People tend to stick with pre-selected or default options.

## Why it matters
Defaults strongly influence user behaviour and outcomes.

## How to apply
- choose defaults carefully
- pre-select the most beneficial or common option
- avoid forcing users to configure everything

## Example
Bad:
All options unselected

Good:
Best option pre-selected

## Fail signs
- users unsure what to choose
- users skip decisions entirely

## Rule
Set the right default and most users will follow it.
