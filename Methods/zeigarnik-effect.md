# Zeigarnik Effect

## What it is
People remember unfinished tasks and feel compelled to complete them.

## Why it matters
Can be used to increase engagement and completion.

## How to apply
- show incomplete states
- highlight unfinished progress
- encourage continuation

## Example
Bad:
No reminder of incomplete work

Good:
“Complete your setup” with progress

## Fail signs
- users don’t return
- users forget unfinished actions

## Rule
Make unfinished work visible and easy to resume.
