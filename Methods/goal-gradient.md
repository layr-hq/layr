# Goal Gradient Effect

## What it is
People move faster as they get closer to a goal.

## Why it matters
Visible progress increases motivation and completion rates.

## How to apply
- show progress (steps, bars, completion)
- break flows into stages
- make progress visible and meaningful

## Example
Bad:
No sense of progress

Good:
Step 2 of 3, progress bar, completion feedback

## Fail signs
- user drops off mid-flow
- user doesn’t know how far they are

## Rule
Always show progress toward completion.
