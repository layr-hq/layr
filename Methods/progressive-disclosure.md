# Progressive Disclosure

## What it is
Showing only what’s needed now, revealing more when required.

## Why it matters
Reduces overwhelm and keeps focus.

## How to apply
- hide advanced options by default
- show details only when relevant
- guide users step by step

## Example
Bad:
All settings shown at once

Good:
Basic view first, advanced options on demand

## Fail signs
- too much information upfront
- users feel overwhelmed early

## Rule
Show less first, reveal more later.
